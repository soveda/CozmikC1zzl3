C1ZZL3 Quick Reference

Startup Envelope Preset Select

Enter:
Hold switch down during startup.
Release the startup hold before selecting or exiting.

Controls:
Main: select envelope preset
LEDs 0-5: selected preset number in binary
Short down press: exit preset select for this session
Long down hold: save selected preset to flash

Presets:
0: Off
1: Pluck
2: Bell
3: Brass
4: Strings
5: Bounce
6: Reverse swell
7: Double pluck
8: Evolving digital

Switch Middle: PD Synth

Controls:
Main: oscillator pitch
X: phase-distortion amount
Y: waveform morph across 8 CZ-inspired waveforms

Inputs:
Audio/CV in 1: pitch modulation
Audio/CV in 2: phase-distortion amount modulation
CV in 1: waveform morph modulation
CV in 2: no normal middle-mode function
Pulse in 1: no synth-mode function
Pulse in 2: triggers the selected envelope preset and softened oscillator sync if preset 1-8 is active
Preset 0 ignores Pulse in 2
Active envelope presets make the oscillator silent between triggers

Outputs:
Audio out 1: oscillator 1
Audio out 2: oscillator 2
CV out 1: scaled stepped Turing CV
CV out 2: smoothed scaled Turing CV
Pulse out 1: main Turing pulse
Pulse out 2: alternate Turing bit pulse


Hold Switch Down: Performance Edit

Controls:
Main: oscillator 2 detune and level from centre
X: latched ring modulation amount
Y: latched CZ-style noise/grit amount

Notes:
Main, X, and Y use soft pickup.
Normal pitch, PD amount, and waveform settings are held while editing.
Hold switch down from middle synth mode for 8 seconds to save latched detune, ring, and noise settings to flash.

Inputs:
CV in 2 positive: increases ring modulation while held
CV in 2 negative: increases noise/grit while held

Outputs:
Same as PD Synth mode


Switch Up: Turing Machine

Controls:
Main: mutation/lock amount; centre is most random, extremes are more locked
X: sequence length, 2 to 16 steps
Y: internal clock speed

Inputs:
Pulse in 1: external Turing clock
Pulse in 2: no Turing-mode function

Outputs:
CV out 1: scaled stepped Turing CV
CV out 2: smoothed scaled Turing CV
Pulse out 1: main Turing pulse
Pulse out 2: alternate Turing bit pulse
Audio out 1: self-playing stepped oscillator 1
Audio out 2: self-playing stepped oscillator 2

Extra:
Flick switch down from Turing mode to tap the internal clock tempo.
The stepped voice uses the last PD synth sound, with the Turing pattern added to pitch.
If an envelope preset is active, the main Turing pulse self-triggers it without oscillator sync.
