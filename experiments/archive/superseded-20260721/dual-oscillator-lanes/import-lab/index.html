Dual Oscillator Lanes Experiment

C1ZZL3 Dual Oscillator Import Lab

Bring in a Casio CZ SysEx patch, decode its amplitude, DCW, and DCO pitch envelopes, then map DCW1/DCW2 and DCO1/DCO2 into separate oscillator lanes in the experimental Envelope Lab.

What it does Decodes CZ patch data including separate DCW and DCO envelopes

Amplitude, DCW1, DCW2, DCO1 pitch, DCO2 pitch, waveform family, and performance values are mapped into a C1ZZL3 draft.

How to use it Send separate PD and pitch lanes

DCW1/PD1 and DCO1 pitch drive oscillator 1; DCW2/PD2 and DCO2 pitch drive oscillator 2 for protocol v4 testing.

Warnings

What may not translate cleanly
  • Waveform behavior is approximate.
  • Pitch envelope decoding is approximate and may need adjustment in Envelope Lab.
  • CZ has separate DCA/DCW/DCO envelopes per synth line; choose line 1, line 2, or merged mappings to compare translations.
  • Modulation details may be dropped in the first pass.
  • Only files that pass the CZ frame checks are treated as draft candidates.
